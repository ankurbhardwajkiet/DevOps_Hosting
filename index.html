Welcome to the Galaxy Explorer

Milky Way

The Milky Way is the galaxy that contains our Solar System. It is a barred spiral galaxy with a diameter between 150,000 and 200,000 light-years.

Andromeda

The Andromeda Galaxy, also known as M31, is the nearest spiral galaxy to the Milky Way. It is on a collision course with the Milky Way and is expected to merge with it in about 4.5 billion years.

Triangulum

The Triangulum Galaxy, also known as M33, is a spiral galaxy approximately 3 million light-years from Earth. It is the third-largest galaxy in the Local Group of galaxies, which includes the Milky Way and Andromeda.
